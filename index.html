Required Student Skills and Qualities

Core Competencies Definition
Ability to combine knowledge Students who have the abilities to combine knowledge of business administration and information system, and think in an integrated way
Ability to think innovatively Students who have the ability to think innovatively in order to solve a variety of complicated problems using diverse information technology
Ability to apply theory to practice Students who have the abilities to use informational system, and to apply theoretical knowledge to real work
Challenging Spirit Attitude to respond to the difficulties encountered in reality in order to achieve his/her goals of life
Future Insights Ability to grasp and predict the future based on both pastand current experiences and knowledge
